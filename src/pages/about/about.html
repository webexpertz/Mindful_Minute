about

Yessica Avancena, the creator of the Mindful Minute App, is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist and Certified Mindfulness Teacher. She is also the co-Founder of Aspire Behavioral Health and MindfulYou Psychotherapy in California where she created the Mindfulness Curriculum for Healing and teaches meditation practices. Her experience includes both private clinical practice and corporate leadership where she held various directorship roles and led large teams and clinics. Yessica has also been a leader in the non-profit and banking industries before her engagement in the mental health field.

For the past decade, she has been passionate about the field of Mindfulness and the healing arts. She is a UCLA-trained Mindfulness Facilitator and Certified by the International Mindfulness Teachers Association. She has also received additional training in Reiki Practices, Shamanic Teachings, and Energy Work. Utilizing her formal clinical knowledge along with alternative healing methods, she uses her skills as an intuitive healer and empath to help individuals along their journey to advance human consciousness one individual at a time.
